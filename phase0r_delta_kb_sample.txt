SCOPE RULES (enforced by the agent prompts)
  - VISUAL SYMPTOMS ONLY. Agents describe what is visible and compare
    it to canonical visual_symptoms. No pathogen / disease-type /
    cause / treatment claims (those are Claude's Phase 1 job).
  - GENERALIZED. No crop/disease-specific forks. DR.Arti.docx informs
    the reasoning STYLE only (short discriminative visual CoT), not a
    literal pipeline.
  - KB-GROUNDED. A delta is emitted only where the photo ADDS to or
    CONTRADICTS canonical visual_symptoms; restating canonical is
    forbidden.
