THE DESIGN (DR.Arti-style decision tree)
  One Bugwood photo = one organ. Detect it once, then activate ONLY
  that organ's DEEP single-feature specialists (the original 24, now
  gated) + always-on cross-cutters. Not all agents fire — only the
  routed branch.

  Cross-cutters on EVERY route (apply to any organ):
    ColorPaletteAgent  (the dedicated colour agent)
    SeverityVisualAgent
    LookAlikeCoTAgent   (look-alike decision graph)
    SporulationAgent    (pathogen signs)

  Routes:
    leaf        -> 8 leaf specialists + concentric + 4 cross = 13
    stem        -> 4 stem specialists + 4 cross               = 8
    root        -> RootAgent + 4 cross                         = 5
    crown       -> CrownCollarAgent + 4 cross                  = 5
    flower      -> FlowerAgent + 4 cross                       = 5
    fruit       -> FruitAgent + concentric + 4 cross           = 6
    whole_plant -> wilting+defoliation+spatial + 3 cross       = 7
    other       -> all 24 (safe full-coverage fallback)

  Visual-symptoms only. Every routed agent compares the photo to the
  canonical visual_symptoms slice; none emits pathogen / type /
  treatment (Claude Phase 1's job). Fully generalized — no
  crop/disease-specific forks.
